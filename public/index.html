Our Story

Milling With Purpose Since 2005

What started as a single portable sawmill and a passion for timber has grown into a full-service milling operation. We source logs locally, mill them on-site, and kiln-dry every board to ensure stability and beauty.

We believe everyone deserves access to stunning, high-quality wood — not just commercial buyers. That's why we sell direct to homeowners, hobbyists, furniture makers, and anyone with a vision.

Frequently Asked Questions

What species of wood do you carry?

We regularly stock black walnut, cherry, hard maple, white oak, red oak, ash, and sycamore. We also get occasional loads of elm, hackberry, spalted maple, and other specialty species. If you're looking for something specific, reach out and we'll do our best to source it.

Is your wood kiln dried?

Yes. All of our lumber is kiln-dried to 6–8% moisture content, which is ideal for indoor furniture and woodworking. We also have air-dried stock available for outdoor projects.

Can I visit and pick out my own slabs?

Absolutely — we encourage it! Our yard is open Monday through Saturday. Walk through our inventory, pick the exact pieces you want, and we'll load them up for you.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes. We offer local delivery within 50 miles and can arrange freight shipping for larger orders nationwide. Smaller items like cutting board blanks and cookies can be shipped via UPS/FedEx.

What are your prices like?

Because we mill everything ourselves and sell direct, our prices are typically 30–50% less than retail lumber dealers. Pricing varies by species, size, and grade. Contact us for a quote on your specific project.

Can you mill my logs?

Yes! Bring your logs to us or we can pick them up (within our service area). We charge by the board foot for custom milling. It's a great way to turn a fallen backyard tree into something beautiful.

How long does kiln drying take?

Typically 2–6 weeks depending on species and thickness. If you're ordering fresh-milled stock, factor in drying time. We always have pre-dried inventory on hand for faster turnaround.

Do you do any finishing work?

We offer flattening, sanding, and basic finish application (oils, polyurethane) as add-on services. We can also cut slabs to size and add features like live edge cleanup and bark removal.
